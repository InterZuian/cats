Interesting Cat Facts

Here are some fun facts about cats:

  • Cats can rotate their ears 180 degrees.
  • A group of cats is called a clowder.
  • Cats sleep for 70% of their lives.
  • They can make over 100 different sounds.
  • The world's oldest cat lived to be 38 years old!
  • Cats can’t taste sweetness.
  • Cats use their whiskers to measure spaces.
  • Cats have a strong sense of smell.
  • Cats can be left or right-pawed.
  • The cat's brain is similar to a human's.
  • Cats can’t see directly under their nose.
  • Cats have been domesticated for thousands of years.
